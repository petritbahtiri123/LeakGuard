Local-only scan. Files are not uploaded, sent to APIs, or stored by LeakGuard.

Choose a text file, text PDF, DOCX, XLSX, or image

Text files, text PDFs, DOCX text, XLSX spreadsheet text, and PNG/JPG/JPEG/WEBP images can be scanned locally and exported as redacted text copies. Image OCR is English-only, runs only after you select an image and click Scan File, and is limited to image files on this scanner page. Protected-site upload OCR is available only when enabled in settings and exports sanitized redacted text. XLSX formulas are scanned as text only and are not executed. Scanned PDF OCR, image redaction, image rebuild, macros, legacy XLS, XLSM, embedded media, archive, executable, and binary-file OCR are not enabled in this release.
